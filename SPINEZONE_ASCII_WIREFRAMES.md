## KEY DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Touch Targets & Spacing
- **Minimum touch target:** 44px x 44px
- **Recommended CTA size:** 56px height 
- **Mobile spacing:** 16px standard, 24px between sections
- **Card spacing:** 16px gaps
- **Content padding:** 20px horizontal

### Color System
- **Primary CTA:** #2563EB (Blue)
- **Secondary CTA:** #059669 (Green) 
- **Urgent/Limited:** #DC2626 (Red)
- **Background:** #F9FAFB (Light gray)
- **Text:** #374151 (Dark gray)

### Typography
- **Mobile headings:** 28px-32px (H1), 24px (H2), 20px (H3)
- **Desktop headings:** 48px-64px (H1), 36px (H2), 28px (H3)
- **Body text:** 16px mobile, 18px desktop
- **CTA text:** 16px mobile, 18px desktop, bold weight

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile:** 320px - 768px (stacked layouts)
- **Tablet:** 768px - 1024px (2-column grids)
- **Desktop:** 1024px+ (multi-column layouts)

### CTA Strategy
- **Primary:** "Schedule Your Appointment" 
- **Secondary:** "Book Appointment Now"
- **Assessment:** "Take Assessment"
- **Service-specific:** "Schedule [Service] Appointment"
- **Placement:** Hero, 50% scroll trigger, section endings, sticky mobile

This comprehensive wireframe system provides the complete visual structure for implementing the shortened homepage and new dedicated pages with mobile-first, conversion-focused design principles.
